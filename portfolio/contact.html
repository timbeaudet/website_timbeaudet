After years spent attempting, Tim Beaudet started making actual games around 2004. He worked in the industry for several years including games like Rock Band 3 by Harmonix and Darkest of Days by 8monkey Labs. His passion for the outdoors brought him on a 2178 mile adventure, hiking from Georgia to Maine along the Appalachian Trial.

He typically works in C++, although has been known to write in Lua, ActionScript3, Python, C# and other languages. With the help of his #GameDevPet's, including Sara cat, Monky bird, Zeus the bearded dragon and Yoshi the vieled chameleon, he is developing a realistic racing simulation and other random games with his 2D game development framework TurtleBrains.

Tim may be half robot, though that has yet to be proven.
