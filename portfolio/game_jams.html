Portal Miner (LD32)
by timbeaudet, Sonia Augier, Jeremy Bell

A mineral miner of the future, traveling to distant asteroid fields to collect resources. The slow collection process got you thinking and you unleash a weapon; the portal. The portal allows you to collect and process resources immediately without the long return trip. Your job is still dangerous, though, so avoid the asteroids and collect the best minerals!
