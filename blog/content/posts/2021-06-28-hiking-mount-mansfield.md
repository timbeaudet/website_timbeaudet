<span style="font-weight: 400;">I met a few hikers walking down. They must have started early I thought. Perhaps a few were hiking the Long Trail, but others seemed to be day hikers, maybe trying to get the sunrise from the peak? I continued up, and up. Step by step.</span>

<span style="font-weight: 400;">The climb really began. It got technical and required much use of hands and careful planning and thought.
