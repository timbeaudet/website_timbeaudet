## What was Learned

### Skill Building Increases Motivation

<!-- /wp:post-content -->

<!-- wp:paragraph --> As mentioned at the end of April a work-work trip derailed my indie productivity and it was hard to get back on track. Grabbing the FGGS training program helped lift motivation with the potential learning. During the month I also started skill building art and typing skills fairly heavily. This should help the indie adventure by having better looking art and efficiency at typing will speed up some parts of development.
